# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We — contributors, maintainers, and autonomous agents acting on our behalf — pledge to make participation in CHERENKOV a harassment-free, respectful, and **honest** experience for everyone.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- **Intellectual honesty:** showing raw evidence, never fabricating results, completeness, or test outcomes. (This is a hard requirement here — see `docs/HANDOVER.md`.)

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- **Fabricating evidence, status, or "done" claims**, or knowingly drifting from the SSOT.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope
Applies within all project spaces (issues, PRs, commits, discussions) and when representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to the repository owner (**@moaidmoatasem**). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate.
